About the Photographer

My name is Codi Ashbacher. I grew up on a ranch near Arrowwood, Alberta with my family of six. I have always loved photography ever since I was given my first ever digital camera. I have enjoyed taking photos of the various animals on my farm, people, and nature. In high school my love of photography grew when I took a photography course allowing me to practice my skills and use a more advanced camera.

I am now enrolled at the University of Calgary in Biological Sciences where I plan to continue on towards my goal of becoming a Veterinarian. I continue to also follow my passion of photography as I travel and experience new things in my life.
